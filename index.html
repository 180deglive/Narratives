The Solution: Qualification System

This three-part system is designed to automate your workflow, giving you back your time and ensuring a seamless, professional experience for every potential client.

Centralized Inquiry Hub & Lead Qualification

This is the new "front door" to your business. We'll implement an all-in-one system to act as a unified CRM, capturing all inquiries into one database. Automated webforms will handle initial qualification, ending manual note-taking and guesswork.

Primary Tool: HoneyBook

An all-in-one system to manage leads, projects, proposals, and payments. Ideal for simplifying your entire clientflow.

Est. Cost: ~$36-59/month

Alternative: Pipedrive

A highly visual, sales-focused CRM excellent for tracking deals through a pipeline. Great for user-friendliness.

Est. Cost: ~$15-30/user/month

Integration: Zapier/Make

Connects social media DMs and other apps to your CRM, automating lead capture from all channels.

Est. Cost: ~$9-50/month

Automated Sales Process & Scheduling

For leads that qualify, we'll create an automated journey. Nurture sequences will build trust, and an automated scheduling tool will handle booking your strategy sessions, including pre-payments, without any back-and-forth emails.

HoneyBook Scheduler

Built directly into HoneyBook, this tool lets clients self-book and pay for sessions, syncing directly with your calendar.

Cost: Included in HoneyBook

Mailchimp (Reactivated)

For more complex email nurturing, your existing Mailchimp account can be integrated to send targeted follow-up campaigns.

Cost: Free tier available

Streamlined Payment & Onboarding

Create a seamless final step. Your all-in-one system will handle proposals, contracts, invoicing (replacing Invoice Home), and payment processing. Once a client is signed, an automated onboarding sequence will deliver welcome materials and set clear expectations.

HoneyBook Clientflow

Consolidates proposals, contracts, invoicing, and payments into one smooth, professional process for your clients.

Cost: Included in HoneyBook

Stripe Integration

Securely process online payments and deposits. Integrates seamlessly with systems like HoneyBook.

Cost: ~2.9% + 25¬¢ per transaction
